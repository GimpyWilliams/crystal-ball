deity_detail with an empty selector times out

Symptom
Calling deity_detail('') (empty selector) raises TimeoutError: timed out from the DFHack socket. Reproducible, not transient — confirmed by an isolated retry. Surfaces in mcp_server.py --selftest as a traceback in the deity_detail('') section.
Location
legends.py:90 — fetch_deity().
Likely cause
Same class as the (now-fixed) stock timeout: a heavy scan exceeding the 5 s socket timeout (dfhack_rpc/client.py:53). An empty selector appears to fall through to scanning all deities / historical figures rather than a single resolved one.
Scope
Unrelated to the stock-inventory work — legends.py was not touched by that change. A non-empty/valid deity selector is the normal call path and is not known to be affected.
Possible fix (not yet applied)
Either reject/short-circuit an empty selector before the live query, or apply the same index-range pagination used for the full stock scan (scripts/stock_query.lua + pipelines.py _fetch_stock_full) to the legends/historical-figure scan.
